## closing-thesis
type: thesis-reveal

### thesis
Through the hunter-prey motif, Ocean Vuong's On Earth We're Briefly Gorgeous exposes the majoritarian American demand that marginalized bodies become legible on dominant terms, analyzes visibility itself as both the precondition of love and the mechanism of harm, and challenges that demand by constructing, in the letter's paratactic form, a self legible only on its own terms.
